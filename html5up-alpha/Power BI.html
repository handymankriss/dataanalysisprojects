Techtronix Innovations

Techtronix Innovations is a forefront player in the microchip and robotics industry. Using Power BI I uncovered sales trends across various product categories, sectors, regions, and timeframes, highlighting the best and worst performers. I was also able to reveal purchase patterns, preferences by sector, and geographic distribution. Identifying the sectors contributing most significantly to sales and profitability. Inventory management against sales figures to pinpoint production planning mismatches, was also evaluated. I also investigated the profitability across different product categories, taking into account sales revenue and production costs. Pointed out products with high margins and suggested cost reduction strategies. Sales and customer data was also analyzed to discover new markets for expansion, emphasizing regions and sectors with untapped potential.
